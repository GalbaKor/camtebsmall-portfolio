About Me:

UX/UI and Front-end web designer living in Bristol, UK.

With a background in philosophy and literature and a love for technology, I am a creative and critical thinker who enjoys building interactive, informative and emotive digital experiences to improve the lives of others. I am confident researching and writing for projects while persevering through problems by learning new techniques and solutions.

I am currently specialising in UX and web Front-End design and development. I am experienced in creating websites using libraries and frameworks such as React JS, Bootstrap and Tailwind. I have experience in all aspects of the User Experience pipeline from ideation and user research to prototyping solutions.
